About TTC

TeaTrendz Café is not just a place to sip tea; it's an experience that transcends the ordinary. Step into our cozy café and immerse yourself in a world of tantalizing aromas and rich flavors.
Our carefully curated selection of teas fromaround the globe ensures there's somethingfor every tea lover.
Whether you prefer theboldness of black tea, the elegance of greentea, or the soothing qualities of herbalblends, TeaTrendz Café has it all.

TeaTrendz

Teatrendz Cafe opened its doors in 2005 with a simple mission: to provide a cozy, welcoming space where people could enjoy high-quality tea in a relaxed atmosphere. What started as a small cafe quickly grew in popularity, thanks to its unique blends and commitment to customer satisfaction.
Over the years, Teatrendz Cafe has become known for its extensive selection of teas from around the world, sourced directly from tea gardens known for their exceptional quality. Whether you're a seasoned tea enthusiast or new to the world of tea, our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to help you find the perfect blend to suit your taste.
In addition to our wide range of teas, Teatrendz Cafe also offers a variety of light bites and pastries, perfect for pairing with your favorite brew. Our cafe has become a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike, seeking a moment of calm in their busy day.
As we look to the future, Teatrendz Cafe remains committed to our founding principles of quality, service, and community. We invite you to join us for a cup of tea and experience the Teatrendz difference for yourself.

FAQ

1. What types of tea do you offer?

We offer a wide range of teas, including black, green, white, herbal, and flavored teas. Our selection includes both classic blends and unique, artisanal teas sourced from around the world.


2. Do you serve food besides tea?

Yes, we offer a selection of light bites and pastries that pair perfectly with our teas. Our menu includes sandwiches, salads, and desserts.


3. Do you have options for people with dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer a variety of options for people with dietary restrictions, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please ask our staff for more information.


4. Do you offer a loyalty program?

Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards frequent customers with discounts and special offers. Ask our staff for more information on how to join.


5. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased in-store and used for any of our products or services.
